Client Art Website

This is my most recent project. A work colleague (Jean) recently requested that I create her a new website for her art business. Her previous website was outdated and did not accurately capture the beauty of her art. The website also did not function on mobile or tablet, which made it hard for Jean to show off her creations to potential clients on the go. I re-created Jean's website from the ground up, giving it a fresh look and design, which better showed off her creations and ensured that the website worked across all device types. I tried to keep the website as simple and user friendly as possible, given Jean's target demographic and included contact links for Instagram, E-mail and mobile, rather than use Javascript, as Jean informed me that this is where most of her business came from.
